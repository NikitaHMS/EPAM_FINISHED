***** - example 
min > hour > day of month > month > day of week

* - 'any'
Example: ***** - every minute of every hour of of every day of every month of every day of the week

*/n - every n
Example: */5**** - every 5 minutes of every hour of of every day of every month of every day of the week

Other examples:
0 */2 * * mon-fri - every two hours at the top of the hour every day of every month from Monday to Friday

0 0 1,10,15 * * - on midnight of every 1st, 10th & 15th day of every month

@midnight - some time between 12:00 AM and 2:59 AM
